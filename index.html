About us

Muslim Connect is a platform dedicated to bridging the gap between travelers and the Muslim community by providing curated information about halal restaurants, mosques, and accommodations. Our goal is to simplify your journey while ensuring comfort and inclusivity. This website was developed as part of an academic project, aiming to showcase web development skills and promote cultural understanding.
